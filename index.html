Who We Are

The Math Society is a student-led organization dedicated to fostering a love for mathematics across all disciplines. We believe that math is not just a subject, but a language that describes the universe.

Whether you are a math major or just curious about the logic behind the world, our doors are open. We provide a supportive community for peer tutoring, research collaboration, and social gatherings.

  • Research Seminars
